The Substance

What would you do to keep and relive your youth? Thats what Hollywood Star Crystal(Demi Moore). On top of the Hollywood fitness videos, her star was at the top for decades. But when her 50th birthday comes around, her world is forever changed. Since she is older, they fired her which left her directionless... that is until a nurse slips her a not saying it can get better with a usb that says "the Substance" on it and phone number.. thats when things start to get crazy.

SMILE 2

A sequal that has been highly anticipated, Smile 2 picks back up from the first movie. The protagonist is a evil entity, and once you see someone smile and kill themselves infront of you, the demon is passed onto that person. Somehow the demon is able to get attached to a troubled Pop Star that is starting a new redemption tour after past struggles. She doesn't know what is happening and has 7 days to get rid of the entity. Can she make it or will be left smiling..

The she starts off following the effects of Matt Reeves, "The Batman" where Gotham is underwater and now is an opportunous time to try for a power grab betweeen the old state.
